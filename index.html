About me

Hi, my name is Oleksandr, and I'm a self-taught web-developer with a great passion for coding and technology. My journey into programming have begun almost a year ago with typing two well-known for every beginner words - "Hello world", and since there I couldn't imagine my life without coding.

During this time I have learnt HTML, CSS, JavaScript, some popular frameworks, including Bootstrap 4, jQuery and practiced my newly acquired knowledge by developing pet projects, that are represented below in portfolio section. Also I've been learning ReactJS, CSS preprocessors (SCSS, LESS), responsive design, git and polished my skills on different helpful websites, like fCC, codecademy, HTMLacademy, etc.

Regarding my lifestyle, I like fishing, reading, listen to music, cycling and travelling. My ultimate dream is to visit every country in Europe, and who knows, maybe every country in the world. I am big fan of traditional competitive sports and e-sports, and really enjoy playing football with my buddies. As for my personal qualities, I'm a simple, modest, friendly, guy open to critics or different views. Additionaly, I am positioning myself as team player, and there is no problem for me to put interests of team above my own.
